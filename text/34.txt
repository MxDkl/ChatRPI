The Corporate Ambassador Program is an initiative designed to foster comradery among Rensselaer alumni and alumnae, and deepen their level of engagement with their alma mater. Corporate Ambassadors are active participants in Rensselaer events and programming, and committed to growing the Rensselaer community within the region and within their company.As a Corporate Ambassador, you pledge to:CORPORATE AMBASSADOR COMMUNITIESIf you are interested in connecting with alumni/ae at your company, staying informed on the latest research and activities at Rensselaer, and have more than 50 Rensselaer alumni/ae at your company, then consider establishing a corporate ambassador community.The Office for Corporate Relations seeks to partner with alumni/ae leaders and Corporate Ambassadors to create an active alumni/ae community at your workplace.
