Learning Objectives:

Researchers will understand the importance of reflection even when it is not associated with a grade
Researchers will learn how to reflect on both their end product and their research process
Researchers will recognize that procrastination tends to be driven not by general laziness but by specific things that cause anxiety
Researchers will learn to use reflection to cut down on their mistakes and increase their personal satisfaction when doing scholarly research
